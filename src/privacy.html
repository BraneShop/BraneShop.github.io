This Privacy Policy discloses how we gather use and protect your personal data.

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data collected by Braneshop Pty Ltd through its website, braneshop.com.au, or, as described below, by other means during the course of its business.

About this document

1. Our broad goals

Ultimately, the Braneshop is interested in building AI literacy and adoption; we are not interested in "behavioural surplus" — behavioural data as a product in-and-of-itself — and make no effort, outside of standard analytics tools such as Google and Facebook, to determine, infer, or derive information that you have not explicitly provided.

Our overall aim with any data collection is:

  1. Selling tickets,
  2. Understanding industry trends and specific needs of specific customers, so-as to develop applicable content for our workshops and website.

We want to run more workshops, and make sure they satisfy the needs of the industry

2. Personal Information

If you fill out a form, subscribed to our newsletter, email us, attend a workshop, or otherwise contact us, we will collect the email address you nominate, and any other identifying information you provide, such as your name, phone number or employer.

Please do not give us other personal or sensitive information such as passwords, or credit card numbers.

The information we collect and use

3. Website analytics

This website may use 'cookies'. Cookies are an industry standard and most major websites use them. A cookie is a small text file that our site may place on your computer as a tool to remember your preferences and identify your browsing behaviour. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser.

You can find more information about cookies at allaboutcookies and youronlinechoices.

Our website uses Google Analytics, a service which transmits website traffic data to Google servers in the United States. Google Analytics does not identify individual users or associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. We use reports provided by Google Analytics o help us understand website traffic and webpage usage.

By using this website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner describe in Google's Privacy Policy and for the purposes set out above. You can opt out of Google Analytics if you disable or refuse the cookie, disable JavaScript, or Use the opt-out service provided by Google. You also consent to the processing of data about you by Facebook in the manner described in Facebook's Privacy Policy. You can update your consent within your Facebook Account, here.

We're tracking your engagement with us via cookies, and we use Google and Facebook trackers, which you can opt out of

4. How we deal with complaints and requests

You may request access to Personal Information about you that we hold and you may ask us to correct your Personal Information if you find that it is not accurate, up-to-date, or complete. You may also make a complaint about our handling of your Personal Information. These services are free of charge.

To protect your privacy and the privacy of others, we will need evidence of your identity before we can grant you access to information about you or change it.

You can contact us by email, or complaint to the postal address below. We undertake to respond within 30 days. If the request or complaint will take longer to resolve, we will provide you with a date by which we expect to respond.

We'll do our best to make changes and enact reasonable requests

5. How we protect your Personal Information

Our forms are processed by Typeform, and Google Forms, and the data is kept on their servers, and may be linked to our Google Drive account and stored on Googles servers. We may bring data down to company computers while processing it.

Access to your Personal Information is restricted to employees who need it to provide services to you.

We typically store it all in third-party secure services, such as Google, to avoid managing it ourselves

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time and will note the date of any updates. In the event of any material and significant changes we will endeavor to provide a more prominent notice of the changes to this Privacy Policy.

When we change this policy, we'll make it clear what's new
