üåà Our Values

"AI has the capability of providing something for everybody, only because, and only when, it is created by everybody.‚Äù ~ Jane Jacobs (She said "cities" instead of "AI", but it's well-expressed!)

Empathy & Ethics

The software we write and algorithms we deploy will affect people. We aim to be aware of potential impacts and create a culture of empathetic ethical engineering.

Community & Diversity

We want to create a positive impact in our local community. We believe that change is best driven locally, upwards.

Empowerment

Skills allow for empowerment and can yield independence. By building technical skills, we hope to enable others to make the kind of change they want to see; change that benefits them and their community.

Happiness

Learning should be fun! We want people to come away from this workshop feeling excited, happy and motivated to implement new systems and processes.

Adaptability

Each group of people comes to the Braneshop with different skills, interests and abilities. We aim to be adaptable to the people we work with, to engage with each individual on a personal level, and present the material in a way that is meaningful, relatable and intuitive.

Technical Excellence

Programming is how we build deep learning networks, and as such we believe it is important to ensure that they way we write and structure the code is of a high quality. We spend a lot of time making sure that we communicate ideas in code through strong coding practices, which ensures that students have a strong foundation on which to build future projects.
